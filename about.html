Our Story

How a mango-season idea grew into a Richmond pantry for Andhra pickles, podi, sweets, putharekulu, and seasonal fruit.

Our story started with a memorable drive to Washington, D.C.

It all began in 2020 in Richmond, Virginia, with a simple mission — to bring the authentic taste of sweet Indian mangoes to our local community. That mango-season search became the starting point for SHRISH: a small business focused on familiar Indian flavors, careful sourcing, and food that feels connected to home.

One day, I drove to Washington, D.C., and finally found a vendor selling real Indian mangoes — the kind that instantly took me back to childhood summers. That moment sparked an idea: Why not bring these flavors directly to Richmond and beyond?

Since then, our journey has grown beyond mangoes into Andhra-style pickles, podi, putharekulu, jelly sweets, snacks, and festive pantry items — while still staying true to the seasonal mango varieties our community waits for each year.

At the heart of our business is one simple motto: "Customer First." We prioritize quality, clear communication, and dependable sourcing so every order feels cared for.

This is more than just a business. It's a story of nostalgia, quality, and serving our community with love — one flavor at a time.
