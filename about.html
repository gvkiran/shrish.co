Our Story

How a craving for home turned into Richmond's favorite source for authentic Indian mangoes.

Our story started with a memorable drive to Washington, D.C.

It all began in 2020 in Richmond, Virginia, with a simple mission — to bring the authentic taste of sweet Indian mangoes to our local community. As an Indian parent, I was searching everywhere for mangoes that tasted like the ones I grew up with — juicy, fragrant, and truly sweet. But all I found were bland, overpriced options that disappointed my kids and reminded me how far from home we really were.

One day, I drove to Washington, D.C., and finally found a vendor selling real Indian mangoes — the kind that instantly took me back to childhood summers. That moment sparked an idea: Why not bring these mangoes directly to Richmond and beyond?

Since then, our journey has grown. From Richmond, we expanded into Norfolk, Roanoke, and Fredericksburg — always staying true to our roots. We've introduced multiple mango varieties beloved by our Indian community, delivering not just fruit, but a slice of home.

At the heart of our business is one simple motto: "Customer First." We prioritize quality and ensure fast, direct-from-farm sourcing, so every box of mangoes tastes just as sweet and fresh as it should.

This is more than just a business. It's a story of nostalgia, quality, and serving our community with love — one mango at a time.
