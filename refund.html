Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Last updated: April 2026

💵 All purchases at Shrish LLC are cash-only at pickup. No online payments are processed, which means there are no card charges to dispute or reverse.

1. Our Freshness Commitment

We source our mangoes in small batches directly from trusted farm suppliers and prepare each order fresh. Your satisfaction with the quality of our produce is very important to us. If you are unhappy with your order for any reason, please contact us — we will do our best to make it right.

2. Inspection at Pickup

We strongly encourage you to inspect your order at the time of pickup before leaving. If you notice any quality issues — bruising, incorrect variety, missing items — please let us know immediately so we can address it on the spot.

3. Eligible Refund Situations

We will offer a full or partial refund/replacement in the following cases:

  • Mangoes received in poor condition (bruised, overripe, or spoiled at time of pickup)
  • Wrong variety or quantity delivered compared to your order
  • Order could not be fulfilled on our end (we will notify you in advance)

4. Non-Refundable Situations

We are unable to offer refunds in the following cases:

  • Mangoes that spoil after pickup due to improper storage (see storage tips below)
  • Change of mind after pickup
  • Failure to pick up your order within the confirmed pickup window without prior notice
  • Dissatisfaction with natural variation in flavor or ripeness (mangoes are a natural product and flavor varies by season and ripeness stage)

5. Order Cancellations

Need to cancel or modify your order? Please contact us as soon as possible:

  • More than 24 hours before pickup: Full cancellation with no issue.
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: We will try our best to accommodate, but may not be able to cancel if the order has already been packed.

To cancel, message us on WhatsApp at +1 (765) 325-5577 or email contact@shrishllc.com.

6. Mango Storage Tips

To get the most out of your purchase:

  • Store unripe mangoes at room temperature until they soften slightly
  • Once ripe, refrigerate and consume within 3–5 days
  • Do not store green mangoes in the refrigerator — it halts ripening
  • Mangoes ripen best at 70–75°F (21–24°C)

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or report a quality issue, contact us within 24 hours of pickup:

  • WhatsApp: +1 (765) 325-5577
  • Email: contact@shrishllc.com

Please include your order ID (found in your confirmation), a brief description of the issue, and a photo if possible. We aim to respond within a few hours.
