Refund Policy

Review pickup orders before leaving and contact us promptly for eligible shipped-order issues

Last updated: July 2026

Important: Checkout may offer pay at pickup for eligible mango carts and online payment for non-mango carts. Refund eligibility depends on order status, fulfillment method, product condition, timing, and applicable law.

1. How Our Business Works

SHRISH LLC operates a preorder business for seasonal food items and specialty products. Orders may be fulfilled by local pickup or, for eligible non-mango items, shipping when checkout shows that option. Pickup refund decisions are tied to inspection at pickup. Shipped-order concerns must be reported promptly after delivery.

2. Inspection at Pickup Is Required

You are responsible for checking your order carefully at pickup before leaving. Please confirm all of the following before you depart:

  • The correct items and varieties were provided
  • The quantity of each item is correct
  • The visible condition of the products is acceptable to you
  • You received the order you intended to pick up

If there is any issue with the order, it must be raised with us at pickup and before you leave the pickup location.

3. No Refund After Leaving Pickup Location

Once you take your order and leave the pickup location, there is no refund. This applies to all items sold by SHRISH LLC. By leaving with the order, you acknowledge that you had the chance to inspect it before departure.

This policy does not limit any rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, including rights relating to unsafe, adulterated, mislabeled, recalled, or legally nonconforming products.

4. Specialty Food Items

The following additional terms apply to sweets, pickles, podi, chutneys, snacks, and other specialty food items sold by SHRISH LLC:

  • Perishable or semi-perishable nature: Once handed over at pickup or delivered by a carrier, shelf life, storage, refrigeration, sealing, transport, and handling become the customer's responsibility.
  • Natural variation: Taste, texture, sweetness, saltiness, spice level, oil content, color, size, and appearance may vary between batches, suppliers, seasons, and handmade products.
  • Handmade items: Products such as Putharekulu and similar traditional sweets may vary in shape, thickness, coating, texture, or finish. Normal artisan variation is not a defect.
  • Oil separation: Pickles, podi, and specialty foods may contain peanut/groundnut oil, sesame oil, coconut oil, or other oils. Oil separation, settling, or variation in oil content is normal and not a defect.
  • Storage guidance: Any storage guidance we provide is general guidance only. Actual shelf life may vary based on temperature, handling, humidity, transport, refrigeration, and other conditions outside our control after pickup.

5. Allergy, Spice, Ingredient, and Image Disclaimers

Customers with food allergies, sensitivities, medical dietary restrictions, pregnancy-related concerns, or spice tolerance concerns must review product information and contact SHRISH LLC before ordering or before accepting the product at pickup. Products may contain or be prepared with peanut/groundnut oil and other major allergens, including milk, egg, fish, crustacean shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soybeans, and sesame.

Products may be manufactured, processed, packed, stored, transported, or handled in facilities or environments that also handle peanuts, tree nuts, milk, eggs, wheat, soy, sesame, fish, and shellfish. Cross-contact may occur even when an allergen is not intentionally listed as an ingredient. If you have a severe or life-threatening food allergy, consult a physician before ordering and contact us before placing an order.

Ingredients, spice level, labels, packaging, and product appearance may vary by supplier batch. Product images are for illustration only. Allergy concerns, spice preference, taste preference, or ingredient questions raised after leaving the pickup location are not eligible for refund unless required by applicable law.

6. What We May Do If an Issue Is Raised Before You Leave

If a genuine issue is identified before you leave the pickup location, we may, at our discretion:

  • Correct a packing error
  • Replace the item if replacement stock is available
  • Offer a partial or full refund for the affected item
  • Move the item to a future shipment, pickup, or delivery cycle if appropriate

These decisions are made case by case based on availability, product condition, and the nature of the issue.

7. Availability, Shipment Delays, and Variety Limits

Some products and varieties depend on incoming shipments and seasonal availability. Placing an order does not guarantee final availability. Some varieties may not arrive even after orders are placed.

In that situation, SHRISH LLC reserves the right to cancel all or part of an order, postpone fulfillment, or try to accommodate the item in a future shipment or next delivery cycle.

8. Pickup Location and Schedule Changes

Pickup locations, dates, and times may change due to operational needs, venue availability, weather, safety concerns, supplier delays, shipment delays, or other factors outside our control. We will make reasonable efforts to notify customers using the contact information on file.

No refund is owed solely because of a pickup location or schedule change if the order remains available for pickup at an updated location or time, unless applicable law requires otherwise.

9. Shipped Orders

Shipping is available only for eligible items when checkout shows shipping as an option. Mangoes are excluded from shipping unless SHRISH LLC clearly states otherwise. For shipped orders, you must contact us promptly after delivery if items are missing, incorrect, damaged, or otherwise materially different from the confirmed order.

We may request photos, packaging details, tracking information, and other reasonable information before deciding whether to replace an item, issue a partial or full refund, provide a credit, or take another corrective step. We are not responsible for spoilage, quality changes, or damage caused by incorrect address entry, carrier delay, missed delivery, weather exposure after delivery, improper storage, or handling after delivery, except where applicable law requires otherwise.

10. No-Show Orders

If you do not appear during the designated pickup time, your order may be marked as a no-show. SHRISH LLC is not obligated to hold, redeliver, or reschedule no-show orders. Customers with repeated no-shows may be restricted from placing future orders, subject to applicable law.

11. Cancellations and Changes Before Fulfillment

If you need to cancel or modify an order before pickup, contact us as early as possible. We will try to accommodate reasonable requests, but once an order has been prepared, packed, or committed for pickup, changes may not be possible.

12. Refusal of Service and Order Cancellation Rights

SHRISH LLC reserves the right to cancel an order, decline service, or refuse a transaction at any time, subject to applicable law. This may occur for availability reasons, operational issues, repeated no-shows, suspected misuse, abusive behavior, inaccurate customer information, or other legitimate business reasons.

13. Recall, Safety, or Supplier Issues

If we learn of a recall, supplier safety notice, contamination concern, allergen issue, regulatory issue, or other product-safety concern, we may cancel orders, remove products, contact affected customers, offer replacement, issue refund, or take other steps we determine are appropriate or legally required.

14. Informal Resolution

If you have a concern about an order, contact us promptly with your order number, pickup location, description of the issue, and any photos or details that may help us review the concern. We will make reasonable efforts to resolve legitimate issues directly before either party considers a formal dispute.
