Refund Policy

Inspection at pickup is required before leaving with your order

Last updated: April 2026

Important: Orders are currently pay at pickup. At pickup, Shrish LLC may accept cash, card, or Zelle. Online payment is not yet enabled on the website, but may be added later.

1. Nature of Our Business

Shrish LLC sells seasonal food items for local pickup. Because these are food products and quality must be checked at pickup, our refund policy is strict and tied to inspection before you leave the pickup location.

2. Customer Inspection at Pickup

You must check your order carefully at pickup before leaving. Please confirm:

  • The correct items and varieties were provided
  • The quantities are correct
  • The visible condition of the products is acceptable to you at pickup

If there is a problem, it must be raised with us at pickup and before you leave the pickup location.

3. No Refund After Leaving Pickup Location

Once you take your order and leave the pickup location, there is no refund. This applies to all items sold by Shrish LLC. By completing pickup, you acknowledge that you had the opportunity to inspect the order before leaving.

4. When We May Help Before You Leave

If an issue is identified before you leave the pickup location, we may, at our discretion:

  • Correct a packing mistake
  • Replace an item if replacement stock is available
  • Offer a partial or full refund for the affected item
  • Move the item to a future shipment or next delivery cycle if appropriate

These decisions are made case by case based on availability, product condition, and the nature of the issue.

5. Availability and Shipment Limits

Because some products and varieties depend on incoming shipments, placing an order does not guarantee final availability. Some varieties may not arrive even after orders are taken. In that situation, Shrish LLC reserves the right to cancel all or part of an order, postpone fulfillment, or try to accommodate the item in a later shipment or next delivery cycle.

6. Cancellations and Modifications Before Pickup

If you need to change or cancel your order before pickup, contact us as early as possible. We will try to accommodate reasonable requests, but once the order is prepared, packed, or committed for pickup, changes may not be possible.

7. Refusal of Service and Order Cancellation

Shrish LLC reserves the right to cancel an order, decline service, or refuse a transaction at any time, subject to applicable law. This may happen for availability issues, repeated no-shows, suspected misuse, abusive behavior, or other business or operational reasons.

8. Contact Us

For pickup issues raised before leaving the pickup location, or for order questions before pickup, contact us at:
