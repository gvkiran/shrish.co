Our Story

A Taste of Home, Delivered

It all began in 2020 in Richmond, Virginia, with a simple mission — to bring the authentic taste of sweet Indian mangoes to our local community. As an Indian parent, I was searching everywhere for mangoes that tasted like the ones I grew up with — juicy, fragrant, and truly sweet.

From Richmond, we expanded into Norfolk, Roanoke, and Fredericksburg — always staying true to our roots. We've introduced multiple mango varieties beloved by our Indian community, delivering not just fruit, but a slice of home.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +
Browse our Shop page, click any mango to view details, add your desired quantity to the cart, then go to checkout. Fill in your name, phone, email, and select a pickup location. No payment needed online — you pay cash at pickup!
Where can I pick up my order? +
We currently have two pickup locations: Short Pump, VA (Richmond West End) and Chesterfield, VA (South of Richmond). The exact address is shared when we call to confirm your order.
How do I pay? +
We collect payment at pickup — cash only. No credit/debit card needed. Please bring exact change or small bills. There is no online payment required when placing your order.
Which mango varieties are currently available? +
Availability changes with the season. Currently, Alphonso (Hapus) and Kesar are available. Visit our Shop page to see live availability — available varieties show first with a green "Available" badge.
How fresh are the mangoes? +
Our mangoes are sourced directly from farms and delivered to Richmond. We order in small batches to ensure maximum freshness. Once you pick up your order, mangoes typically last 3–7 days at room temperature or up to 2 weeks refrigerated.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
Yes! Contact us as soon as possible via WhatsApp at +1 (765) 325-5577 or email [email protected]. Since we prepare orders fresh, modifications are easiest within 24 hours of placing.
Do you deliver to my home? +
Currently we offer pickup only at Short Pump and Chesterfield. Home delivery is coming soon! Join our WhatsApp group to be notified when delivery launches.
Will you carry Indian sweets (Putharekulu)? +
Yes! Authentic GI-tagged Putharekulu from Atreyapuram, Andhra Pradesh is coming soon in multiple varieties — Plain, Sugar+Dry Fruits, Jaggery, and Sugar-Free. Join our WhatsApp group to be first in line!
