Our story

A taste of home, delivered.

It all began in 2020 in Richmond, Virginia, with a simple mission — to bring the authentic taste of sweet Indian mangoes to our local community. As an Indian parent, I was searching everywhere for mangoes that tasted like the ones I grew up with — juicy, fragrant, and truly sweet.

From Richmond we grew into Norfolk, Roanoke, and Fredericksburg — and the pantry grew with us. Today our kitchen crafts small-batch Andhra pickles, podi, and traditional sweets like GI-tagged Putharekulu, shipped to homes across America. Not just fruit — a whole slice of home.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +
Browse our Shop page, click any product to view details, add your desired quantity to the cart, then check out securely online. During mango season (March–June), mango carts can pay at pickup.
Where can I pick up my order? +
We currently have three pickup locations: Short Pump, VA (Richmond West End), Chesterfield, VA (South of Richmond), and Mechanicsville, VA (North of Richmond). The exact address is shared in the whatsapp group.
How do I pay? +
Checkout is secure and online for pickles, podi, and sweets. During mango season (March–June), mango-only carts may also pay at pickup by cash, card, or Zelle.
Which mango varieties are currently available? +
Availability changes with the season. Visit our Shop page to see live availability — available varieties show first with a green "Available" badge.
How fresh are the mangoes? +
Our mangoes are sourced directly from farms and delivered to Richmond. We order in small batches to ensure maximum freshness. Once you pick up your order, mangoes typically last 3–7 days at room temperature or up to 2 weeks refrigerated.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
Yes! Contact us as soon as possible via WhatsApp at +1 (765) 325-5577 or email contact@shrish.co . Since we prepare orders fresh, modifications are easiest within 24 hours of placing.
Do you deliver to my home? +
Yes — we ship pickles, podi, and eligible sweets anywhere in the USA; the shipping option appears at checkout. Richmond locals can instead pick up in Short Pump, Chesterfield, or Mechanicsville. Mangoes are seasonal and pickup only.
Will you carry Indian sweets (Putharekulu)? +
Yes! Authentic GI-tagged Putharekulu from Atreyapuram, Andhra Pradesh is available in selected batches and varieties. Check the sweets section for current availability.
