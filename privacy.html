Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Shrish LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the website shrish.co and sells fresh Indian mangoes and sweets for pickup in Richmond, Virginia. Our contact details:

  • Email: contact@shrishllc.com
  • Phone: +1 (765) 325-5577

2. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we collect only what is necessary to fulfill your order:

  • Name – to identify your order
  • Phone number – to confirm pickup details
  • Email address – to send order confirmation
  • Preferred pickup location – Short Pump or Chesterfield
  • Order details – items and quantities selected

We do not collect payment information. All payments are cash-only at pickup.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your order
  • To contact you about pickup timing and availability
  • To respond to questions or support requests
  • To send occasional updates about new mango varieties (only if you opt in via our WhatsApp group)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Order information submitted through our website is stored in our secure order system powered by Firebase/Firestore so we can manage fulfillment, pickup coordination, and customer communications. We keep only the information needed to process your order and support the customer experience.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal browser storage (sessionStorage) to maintain your cart during your visit. We also use Firebase services for authentication, catalog management, and order submission. No advertising cookies are intentionally used by us. Third-party services embedded on our site (such as Google Maps on the Contact page) may set their own cookies per their respective privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps – for pickup location display
  • WhatsApp – for community group and direct messaging
  • Instagram – for social media presence (@richmond_mangos)
  • Formspree – for contact form submissions

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information
  • Opt out of any future communications

To exercise these rights, email us at contact@shrishllc.com or message us on WhatsApp.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach us at:

  • Email: contact@shrishllc.com
  • Phone / WhatsApp: +1 (765) 325-5577
