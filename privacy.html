Privacy Policy

How SHRISH LLC collects, uses, stores, and protects customer information

Last updated: May 2026

Important: SHRISH LLC currently operates as a pickup-only preorder business. Orders are generally pay at pickup, and we may currently accept cash, card, or Zelle at pickup. Online payment is not yet enabled on the website, but may be added later.

1. Who We Are

SHRISH LLC (“Shrish,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website shrish.co. We offer local preorder and pickup of seasonal food items and specialty products, including mangoes and other items we make available from time to time.

Our business currently serves customers through local pickup in the Richmond, Virginia area, including Short Pump, VA, Chesterfield, VA, and Mechanicsville, VA.

  • Email: contact@shrish.co
  • Phone / WhatsApp: +1 (765) 325-5577

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account, place an order, contact us, join our communication channels, or otherwise interact with our website, we may collect information needed to run the business and support your order, including:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your selected pickup location
  • Your optional address if you choose to save it in your account
  • Your saved account profile details and preferred pickup location
  • Your order details, including items, quantities, and notes
  • Basic session or browser information required to maintain cart and checkout functionality
  • Customer communication history related to your orders, inquiries, or support
  • Account authentication identifiers and account activity needed to support customer accounts
  • Messages, inquiries, or communications you send us through the website, email, phone, SMS, WhatsApp, or other customer support channels

We do not store your account password in our database. Password login is handled by Firebase Authentication. We collect only the information reasonably needed to process orders, coordinate pickup, respond to customers, and operate the business.

3. Customer Accounts and Order History

Customer accounts are optional. If you create an account, you may be able to view order history, save basic profile details, and manage eligible pending orders before they are finalized.

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure and for notifying us promptly if you believe your account has been accessed without authorization. We may suspend or restrict accounts associated with misuse, suspected fraud, repeated no-shows, abusive conduct, or inaccurate customer information, subject to applicable law.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use customer information for normal business and operational purposes, including:

  • Receiving and organizing preorder requests
  • Confirming or updating order availability
  • Coordinating pickup details and customer communication
  • Sending order confirmations and related updates
  • Responding to customer questions and support requests
  • Managing cancellations, substitutions, delays, and fulfillment issues
  • Maintaining internal order records and business records
  • Improving our ordering process and customer experience

By placing an order or providing your contact information, you authorize SHRISH LLC to contact you regarding order updates, customer communication, and informational purposes related to your order or service experience.

5. WhatsApp, SMS, Phone, and Email Communications

We may communicate with customers through email, phone calls, SMS/text messages, WhatsApp direct messages, WhatsApp groups, or similar channels. Order-related communications may include order confirmations, pickup details, availability changes, substitutions, reminders, cancellation notices, customer support, and safety or recall-related notices.

Promotional or marketing-style SMS, WhatsApp, or email messages will be handled separately from essential order communications. Where required by law, we will use appropriate consent and opt-out methods. You may opt out of non-essential promotional communications by contacting us at contact@shrish.co. For SMS marketing, you may also reply STOP where that option is available. Standard message and data rates may apply.

6. Order Availability, Shipment Variability, and Communication

Some products and varieties sold by SHRISH LLC depend on seasonality, supplier availability, and incoming shipments. Because of that, placing an order does not guarantee that every item or variety selected will be available at fulfillment time.

In some cases, a variety may not arrive even after orders are placed. In those situations, we may contact you to provide an update, discuss alternatives if available, fulfill later if feasible, or cancel all or part of the order.

7. Payment Information

At this time, online payment is not yet enabled on our website. Orders are generally completed as pay at pickup. Depending on our current setup, payment at pickup may be accepted by cash, card, or Zelle.

If online payments are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in payment processing and related data handling.

If online payments are introduced in the future, payment information may be processed by a third-party payment processor such as Stripe or another provider. SHRISH LLC does not intend to store full card numbers on its own systems.

8. How We Store and Protect Information

We use operational systems, hosting infrastructure, and service providers to manage customer information, order details, communication, and business operations. We take reasonable steps to protect customer information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no website, system, or method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You use the site and submit information at your own discretion.

Please use care when submitting information online or through messaging channels, and contact us promptly if you believe your account or order information has been accessed without authorization.

9. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited customer information with service providers or technical platforms that support our business operations, such as website functionality, communication tools, mapping, order management, or email delivery, but only to the extent reasonably necessary to operate SHRISH LLC.

Examples of service providers or platforms may include Firebase authentication and database services, Vercel hosting and analytics, email tools, communication tools, mapping tools, order management systems, payment processors if used, and similar operational providers.

We may also disclose information if reasonably necessary to protect our legal rights, enforce our terms, respond to fraud or misuse, or comply with legal obligations.

10. Cookies, Session Data, and Website Functionality

Our website may use browser storage, session-based data, or similar technologies to maintain your cart, support checkout flow, remember temporary selections, and improve site usability.

We also use privacy-conscious website analytics to understand page visits, product interest, cart actions, checkout flow, and clicks to contact channels. We do not intentionally send customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, order notes, or free-form checkout text to analytics tools.

We do not describe the website as using third-party advertising cookies for broad ad-targeting purposes. Some external services or embedded tools may set their own technical cookies or similar data according to their own practices.

11. Customer Choices and Requests

You may contact us to request corrections to your contact details or to ask questions about information you submitted to us. We may retain certain records where reasonably necessary for order history, dispute handling, business records, tax records, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

You may also contact us to request access to or deletion of personal information you submitted to us, or to request account deletion. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request, and we may retain records where necessary for order history, disputes, tax, accounting, food-safety or recall records, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

12. Marketing and Informational Communications

When you place an order or provide your information to SHRISH LLC, you understand that we may use your contact information for order-related communication and informational business communication connected to your customer experience.

If we send broader promotional or marketing-style updates in the future, we may provide additional choices where appropriate.

You may opt out of non-essential promotional communication by contacting us at contact@shrish.co. Order-related and safety-related communications may still be sent where reasonably necessary to complete or service an order.

13. Safety, Recall, and Product Notices

If we learn of a product safety issue, supplier notice, recall, allergen concern, contamination concern, or regulatory issue, we may use customer contact information to notify affected customers, cancel orders, remove products, or take other steps reasonably necessary to protect customers and comply with applicable law.

14. Photography and Social Media

We may photograph product displays or pickup events for legitimate business and marketing purposes. If we want to use an identifiable customer as the main subject of a photo, video, testimonial, or promotional post, we will seek consent where appropriate. If you do not want to be photographed at pickup, please tell us at the pickup location and we will make reasonable efforts to accommodate the request.

15. Virginia Consumer Protection and Privacy Rights

Nothing in this Privacy Policy is intended to waive or limit rights that cannot be waived under applicable federal or Virginia law, including applicable consumer protection or privacy laws. To the extent any provision conflicts with applicable law, applicable law controls.

16. Children’s Privacy

Our website is intended for general business use by adults placing orders. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

17. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, operations, payment methods, website functionality, or legal obligations. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest revision.

18. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how SHRISH LLC handles customer information, contact us at:
